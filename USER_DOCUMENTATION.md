## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the HealthQuest Platform**
   - Navigate to the HealthQuest homepage
   - Click the "Get Started" button

2. **Sign In with Google**
   - You'll be prompted to authenticate using your Google account
   - HealthQuest uses Google OAuth for secure, easy login
   - No need to remember another password!

3. **Choose Your Role**
   - After first-time login, you'll be taken to the Role Selection page
   - Choose between:
     - **Normal User**: Independent fitness journey with self-guided workouts
     - **Member**: Get personalized coaching and guidance from certified trainers
     - **Coach**: Create programs and guide members (requires certification)

4. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Enter your personal information:
     - Height and weight
     - Age and gender
     - Location
     - Upload a profile photo (optional)
   - **For Coaches**: Upload your certification documents for admin approval

5. **Select Your Fitness Goals** (Normal Users & Members)
   - Choose from:
     - Lose Weight
     - Build Muscle
     - Improve Endurance
     - General Fitness
     - Increase Flexibility

6. **Start Your Journey!**
   - You'll be redirected to your personalized dashboard
   - Begin exploring workouts, earning XP, and leveling up!

## Understanding User Roles

### Normal User

**Who is this for?** Individuals who prefer to work out independently and follow their own fitness path.

**What you can do:**
- Access all public workout programs
- Browse and filter workouts by difficulty and category
- Track your progress with the XP system
- Level up to unlock recipe features
- View your workout completion streaks
- Track your fitness journey through analytics

**What you cannot do:**
- Connect with a coach
- Share food posts for feedback
- Access coach-assigned programs

**Best for:** Self-motivated individuals who enjoy creating their own workout routine.

---

### Member

**Who is this for?** Users who want personalized guidance and accountability from a certified fitness coach.

**What you can do:**
- Everything a Normal User can do, PLUS:
- Connect with a certified coach
- Receive coach-assigned workout programs
- Share your meals via Food Posts
- Get personalized nutrition feedback
- Access your private Food Diary
- Build a coach-member relationship

**What you cannot do:**
- View public workout programs (you'll follow coach-assigned programs instead)
- Create workout programs

**Best for:** People who want professional guidance, personalized programs, and accountability.

---

### Coach

**Who is this for?** Certified fitness trainers who want to guide and manage members.

**What you can do:**
- Create custom workout programs
- Manage multiple members
- Assign programs to specific members
- Review and approve member connection requests
- Provide nutrition feedback on member food posts
- Track member progress and completion rates
- Access the Recipe Library
- Manage your coach profile and bio

**Requirements:**
- Valid fitness certification
- Admin approval (required before accessing full features)

**Best for:** Professional trainers looking to expand their digital coaching presence.

---

### Administrator

**Who is this for?** Platform managers responsible for overseeing the entire HealthQuest ecosystem.

**What you can do:**
- Manage all users, coaches, and members
- Review and approve/reject coach applications
- Moderate content (recipes and workout programs)
- Monitor platform statistics and analytics
- Handle user reports and content moderation
- Delete inappropriate content or users
- View comprehensive platform insights
- Access admin-only dashboard and management tools

**Requirements:**
- Assigned admin privileges by system administrators
- Platform management training

**Best for:** Trusted individuals responsible for maintaining platform quality, safety, and integrity.

## The Gamification System

HealthQuest makes fitness fun through an XP (Experience Points) and leveling system!

### How XP Works

**Earn XP by:**
- Completing workout days
- Maintaining workout streaks
- Achieving fitness milestones
- Earning achievements

**XP Progress:**
- Your XP bar shows how close you are to the next level
- Check your dashboard to see current XP and progress percentage

---

### User Levels

#### Bronze Level (0-999 XP)
**Status:** Starting Level (Everyone begins here)

**Features Available:**
- Access to all public workout programs
- Basic dashboard analytics
- Profile management
- Streak tracking

**Limitations:**
- No access to Recipe Library

**How to Advance:** Complete workouts to earn XP! You need 1,000 XP to reach Silver.

---

#### Silver Level (1,000-4,999 XP)
**Status:** Intermediate Level

**New Features Unlocked:**
- Full access to Recipe Library
- View all community-shared recipes
- Download recipes as PDFs
- Enhanced dashboard features

**Limitations:**
- Cannot create or share your own recipes

**How to Advance:** Keep completing workouts! You need 5,000 XP to reach Gold.

---

#### Gold Level (5,000+ XP)
**Status:** Advanced Level

**New Features Unlocked:**
- Create and share your own recipes
- Upload recipe images
- Contribute to the community Recipe Library
- All Silver-level features
- Premium status badge

**Limitations:** None! You have full access to all user features.

---

### Achievements & Streaks

**Workout Streaks:**
- Complete workouts on consecutive days
- Your current streak is displayed on your dashboard
- Longer streaks = bonus XP

**Achievements:**
- Unlock achievement badges by reaching milestones
- Examples: "First Workout", "7-Day Streak", "50 Workouts Completed"
- Achievements earn you bonus XP
- View your achievement collection on your profile

## Workout Programs

### Browsing Workouts

**For Normal Users:**
1. Navigate to "Workouts" from the menu
2. Browse all public workout programs
3. Filter by:
   - Difficulty Level (Easy, Medium, Hard)
   - Category (Strength, Cardio, Weight Loss, etc.)
   - Duration (number of days)

**For Members:**
1. Navigate to "Workouts" from the menu
2. View programs assigned by your coach
3. You'll only see coach-assigned programs (not public ones)

---

### Understanding Workout Cards

Each workout program displays:
- **Title**: Program name
- **Description**: Overview of what the program includes
- **Difficulty**: Easy, Medium, or Hard
- **Category**: Strength Training, Cardio, Weight Loss, Muscle Building, Endurance, Flexibility, Full Body, Upper/Lower Body, Core
- **Duration**: Total number of days
- **Access Level**: Bronze, Silver, or Gold
- **View Program Button**: Click to see full details

---

### Viewing a Workout Program

1. Click "View Program" on any workout card
2. You'll see:
   - Program title and full description
   - Complete day-by-day breakdown
   - Overall program duration

3. Each day includes:
   - Day number (Day 1, Day 2, etc.)
   - Workout title
   - Workout duration in minutes
   - Video tutorial links
   - Workout type description
   - Completion checkbox

---

### Completing a Workout

1. Select the workout day you want to complete
2. Watch any video tutorials provided
3. Follow the workout instructions
4. After completing the workout, check the "Mark as Complete" box
5. Earn XP instantly!
6. Your progress updates automatically
7. Track completion percentage for the full program

**Tips:**
- Follow the days in order for best results
- Don't skip rest days if the program includes them
- Repeat programs to continue earning XP

---

### Workout Categories Explained

- **Strength Training**: Build muscle and increase strength
- **Cardio**: Improve cardiovascular fitness and endurance
- **Weight Loss**: Focus on fat burning and calorie expenditure
- **Muscle Building**: Hypertrophy-focused programs
- **Endurance**: Long-duration stamina building
- **Flexibility**: Stretching, yoga, mobility work
- **Full Body**: Compound movements targeting all muscle groups
- **Upper Body**: Focus on arms, chest, back, and shoulders
- **Lower Body**: Focus on legs, glutes, and core
- **Core**: Abdominal and lower back strengthening

## Food Diary & Posts

**Access Requirement:** Member role (connected to a coach)

### What is Food Posting?

Food posting allows Members to share their daily meals with their assigned coach for personalized nutrition feedback. It's a private, one-on-one communication tool between you and your coach.

---

### Creating a Food Post

1. Navigate to "Food Post" from the menu
2. Fill in the meal details:
   - **Meal Title**: E.g., "Breakfast - Protein Oatmeal"
   - **Description**: Notes about the meal, portion sizes, how you felt
   - **Photo**: Upload a clear photo of your meal

3. Click "Submit"
4. Your post is sent to your coach
5. Wait for coach feedback

---

### Viewing Your Food Diary

1. Navigate to "Food Diary" from the menu
2. See all your previous food posts
3. View coach comments and feedback
4. Track your nutrition patterns over time

**Benefits:**
- Get professional nutrition advice
- Understand what's working and what needs adjustment
- Build awareness of your eating habits
- Stay accountable to your coach

---

### Coach Feedback

- Your coach will review your food posts
- Expect comments about:
  - Portion sizes
  - Macronutrient balance
  - Meal timing
  - Healthy substitutions
  - Hydration reminders
  - Encouragement and support

- Feedback appears as comments on your food posts
- Use feedback to improve your nutrition habits

## Member Features

   - Your experience level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)

---

**Step 4: Wait for Approval**
- Your request is sent to the coach
- Status shows as "Pending"

- You'll be notified when approved or rejected

---

**Step 5: Get Started with Your Coach**

Once approved:
- View your coach's full profile and bio
- See your assigned workout program
- Start following coach-assigned workouts
- Begin sharing food posts
- Communicate with your coach

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is HealthQuest free to use?**
A: Pricing information is managed by the platform administrators. Check the landing page for current pricing details.

**Q: Can I use HealthQuest on mobile?**
A: Yes! HealthQuest is responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

**Q: Do I need special equipment for workouts?**
A: Depends on the program. Check each program's description for equipment requirements. Many programs offer bodyweight-only options.

---

### Account Questions

**Q: Can I change my role after signing up?**
A: Contact admin support to change roles. Note that switching roles may affect your progress and features.

**Q: I forgot to mark my workout as complete. Can I go back?**
A: Yes, you can mark any workout day as complete at any time, even past days.

**Q: How long does coach approval take?**
A: Typically 24-48 hours. Admins review certification documents carefully to ensure quality.

---

### XP & Leveling Questions

**Q: How much XP do I earn per workout?**
A: XP varies based on workout difficulty and duration. Check each workout for specific XP rewards.

**Q: Do I lose XP if I miss days?**
A: No, you never lose XP. However, your streak resets if you miss consecutive days.

**Q: What happens when I level up?**
A: You'll receive a notification, unlock new features, and get a level badge on your profile.

**Q: Can I see how much XP I need to level up?**
A: Yes, your dashboard displays your current XP and progress bar to the next level.

---

### Member Questions

**Q: Can I have multiple coaches?**
A: No, you can only connect with one coach at a time.

**Q: What if my coach doesn't respond to my food posts?**
A: Coaches have 48 hours to respond. If your coach is consistently unresponsive, you can cancel the connection and find another coach.

**Q: Can I still do public workouts as a Member?**
A: No, Members follow coach-assigned programs exclusively for consistency and personalization.

**Q: How do I cancel my coach connection?**
A: Go to "Coach Connect" and click "Cancel Connection". Your data is preserved, and you can connect with a new coach later.

---

### Coach Questions

**Q: How many members can I have?**
A: There's no limit. Manage as many members as you can effectively support.

**Q: Can I charge my members?**
A: HealthQuest handles platform access. External payment arrangements between coaches and members are outside the platform.

**Q: Can I delete programs after assigning them?**
A: Yes, but currently assigned programs will remain accessible to members until they complete them.

**Q: How do I get my Coach ID to share with members?**
A: Your Coach ID is displayed on your Coach Dashboard. Format: C-XXXXX.

---

### Recipe Questions

**Q: Why can't I access recipes at Bronze level?**
A: Recipes are unlocked as a reward for reaching Silver level. Complete workouts to earn XP!

**Q: Can I edit recipes after publishing?**
A: Yes, you can edit your own recipes anytime from "My Recipes".

**Q: Are there nutritional facts on recipes?**
A: Currently, recipes include ingredients and instructions. Nutritional calculations may be added in future updates.

**Q: Can I report inappropriate recipes?**
A: Yes, use the report button on recipe cards. Admins review all reports.

---

### Technical Questions

**Q: The video link isn't working. What should I do?**
A: Ensure the link is a valid YouTube or video hosting URL. If the problem persists, notify your coach or contact support.

**Q: My progress isn't saving. What's wrong?**
A: Check your internet connection. HealthQuest requires an active connection to save progress. Refresh the page and try again.

**Q: Can I use HealthQuest offline?**
A: No, HealthQuest requires an internet connection for all features.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact admin support to request account deletion. Note: This action is permanent.

---

### Administrator Questions

**Q: How do I become an admin?**
A: Admin privileges are assigned by system administrators. Contact your organization's IT department or platform owner.

**Q: What criteria should I use to approve coach certifications?**
A: Verify the certification is from a recognized body (NASM, ACE, ISSA, ACSM, NSCA), check expiration dates, ensure the name matches, and look for authenticity markers. When in doubt, research the certification body.

**Q: Can I undo a user deletion?**
A: No, user deletions are permanent and cannot be reversed. Always double-check before confirming deletions.

**Q: How do I handle inappropriate content?**
A: Review the content, verify it violates platform policies, then delete it and document the reason. For serious violations, consider suspending or banning the user.

**Q: What should I do if a coach has multiple member complaints?**
A: Review the complaints, investigate the coach's activity, check member feedback, and consider suspension while investigating. If violations are confirmed, revoke coach status or permanently remove them.

**Q: Can I edit user profiles or content?**
A: Admin capabilities vary by platform configuration. Typically, you can delete content but should avoid editing user-created content to maintain authenticity. Use deletion for policy violations.

**Q: How often should I review pending coach applications?**
A: Check daily or at least every 24-48 hours to maintain good user experience. Prompt responses encourage quality coaches to join.

**Q: What reports should I monitor regularly?**
A: Focus on User Growth Charts, Engagement Metrics, Coach Performance, Content Moderation status, and Platform Health indicators. Weekly reviews are recommended.

**Q: How do I export platform data?**
A: Navigate to the relevant management section (Users, Coaches, Recipes, Workouts) and click "Export" to download data as CSV for analysis.

**Q: What if I accidentally approve a coach who shouldn't be approved?**
A: You can revoke coach status from the Coach Management section. The coach will be demoted to Normal User status and members will be disconnected.
